About Vitrine

Vitrine is an independent trade publication for the fashion, sport, and luxury retail industries — anchored in Indonesia, with a wider eye on Asia.

It exists because the industry that drives meaningful share of Indonesian consumer spending has, until now, gone uncovered in serious form. Daily reporting catches launches and openings. Vitrine catches the structure underneath them — who distributes what, who owns what, where the money moves, which way.

We publish quarterly reference reports, a weekly briefing, and original analysis between them. We do not chase virality. We write for the readers who already have a stake in the answer.
